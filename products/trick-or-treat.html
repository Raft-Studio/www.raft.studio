Trick or Treat
Game Design: Lidia Tkachenko
Illustrator: Mikolaj Lasota
Number of players:
2-4
Time to play:
10-20 min
Age:
7+
Premise
It’s spooky Halloween time. Take your friends and go play Trick or Treat to get candies but be careful, sometimes it’s not the candy you want. Remember it’s Halloween so your friends become your foes while going candy hunting. Go for your candy! Trick your friends! But watch very closely your friends can play a trick on you!
Components
  • 46 action cards
  • 5 "candy" cards
  • 3 "poop" cards
  • Rules
